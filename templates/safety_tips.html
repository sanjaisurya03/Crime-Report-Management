Safety Tips & Resources

Personal Safety

  • Always stay aware of your surroundings, especially at night.
  • Keep emergency contacts saved in your phone.
  • Avoid sharing personal info with strangers online or offline.
  • Install safety apps like CitizenCop or bSafe.

Home & Neighborhood Safety

  • Ensure all doors and windows have strong locks.
  • Join or form a local neighborhood watch group.
  • Report any suspicious activity to local police immediately.
  • Install security cameras if possible.

Cyber Safety

  • Don't click unknown links or open suspicious attachments.
  • Use strong, unique passwords and enable 2FA wherever possible.
  • Never share OTPs or banking information.
  • Report cybercrime at cybercrime.gov.in.
